Vectorless RAG

No embeddings. No vectors. An LLM expands your query into precise legal terminology, then BM25 scores every document by exact keyword frequency.

When precise wording matters more than meaning, this wins.

Graph RAG

An LLM reads documents and builds a knowledge graph — entities connected by labelled edges. Queries traverse this graph hop by hop.

Best when the answer depends on who is connected to what.

Chapter 4

Agentic RAG

An AI agent plans, selects tools, executes sub-queries, validates confidence, retries when weak, grounds the answer, and only then responds.

The most powerful pattern — and the most expensive.
